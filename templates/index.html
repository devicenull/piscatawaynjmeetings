Piscataway Township, New Jersey doesn't do a great job of publishing their meeting schedule/minutes.
In particular, they don't really publish meeting minutes at all (as of writing 2020-01-05, they only have ~6 township council meetings listed, the rest are unavailable).
Don't see a meeting you want?
Email oprarequests@piscatawaynj.org with something like the following:
Dear Piscataway Township,

This is a request for public records made under OPRA and the common law right of access. I am not required to fill out an official form.

Please acknowledge receipt of this message.

Records requested:

Meeting minutes for (INSERT TYPE HERE) on (INSERT DATE HERE)

I would like these minutes to be delivered by email.

Sincerly,
(YOUR NAME)

Expect their response to take several days at least.
