Payments in Lieu of Taxes (PILOTs)

PILOT agreements allow developers to pay a negotiated annual fee instead of standard property taxes, typically for 30 years. The municipality keeps 100% of the PILOT payment; school and county taxes are not collected on these properties. Data is from the User Friendly Budget filings (2017–present); 2021 data is unavailable because the 2022 budget used a legacy format with no per-project detail.
