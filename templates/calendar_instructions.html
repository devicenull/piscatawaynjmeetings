Adding the Calendar feed to your phone

This process is way harder then it should be!
For Google Calendar (this is easier to do from a computer):
  • Open Google Calendar
  • In the bottom right, click the "+" button next to "Other calendars"
  • Select "From URL"
  • Put 
    http://www.piscatawaynjmeetings.com/ical.php
    into the "URL of calendar" field
  • Click "Add calendar"
